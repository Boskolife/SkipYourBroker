Privacy Policy for SkipYourBroker Platform

Effective Date: 24.01.2024

Welcome to SkipYourBroker (SYB), the cutting-edge platform transforming the car shipping experience. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use the SYB platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information when you use the SYB platform, including but not limited to:

  • Personal Information: Name, contact information, and other identifying details.
  • Vehicle Details: Information about the vehicle(s) you wish to transport.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect login credentials and other account-related information.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Facilitating Car Transportation: Utilizing your provided details to arrange and coordinate the transportation of your vehicle.
  • Improving User Experience: Analyzing usage data to enhance the functionality and user-friendliness of the SYB platform.
  • Communication: Sending updates, notifications, and relevant information about your transportation journey.

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with trusted partners, service providers, and affiliates involved in the transportation process. However, we do not sell or lease your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

SYB employs industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Despite our efforts, no online platform can guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze usage patterns, and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Third-Party Links

The SYB platform may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third parties. Please review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

7. Children's Privacy

The SYB platform is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

SYB reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any significant changes via the platform or other means.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding these Terms of Use, please contact us at contact@skipyourbroker.com

Thank you for entrusting SkipYourBroker with your car shipping needs. We are committed to providing you with a secure and efficient transportation experience.
