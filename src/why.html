FAQs - Your Questions Answered

Can I put personal items in the car?

Yes, you can include personal items weighing up to 100 lbs. in a secure box or a single piece of luggage placed in the trunk during transport.

Is my vehicle insured during transport?

Absolutely. The carrier's cargo insurance covers your vehicle. Additionally, we offer contingent cargo insurance, providing additional coverage of up to $250,000 in case the carrier's policy falls short.

When is the vehicle going to be picked up?

The pickup schedule is determined based on your preferences and the availability of carriers. You'll have control over the pickup timing during the order placement process.

When will my vehicle be delivered?

Delivery times vary based on the destination and other factors. You'll receive an estimated delivery window during the order placement, ensuring transparency throughout the process.

Can I track my vehicle online?

Yes, you can track your vehicle in real time through our user-friendly tracking system. Stay informed about the current location and progress of your shipment with ease.
